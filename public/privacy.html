GhostLog

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026 · Last Updated: March 8, 2026

GhostLog ("we", "the app") is a fitness and nutrition tracking application. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide:

  • Workout logs, meal logs, daily check-in data (weight, steps, sleep, stress, fatigue)
  • Macro targets and phase preferences (cut/bulk/maintain)
  • Photos submitted to the AI food scanner (processed in real-time, not stored on our servers)
  • Text prompts sent to AI features (Ghost Chef, Ghost Report, food search, target calculator)

Data collected automatically:

  • Anonymous Firebase authentication ID (no email or name required)
  • Device platform (iOS, Android, or web)
  • Subscription status via RevenueCat (no payment details are accessible to us)

Data we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (unless you voluntarily link an Apple ID)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendar, or other device data
  • Credit card or payment information (handled entirely by Apple/Google)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality: Your workout and meal data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase cloud backup.
  • AI features: Text prompts and food photos are sent to Google Gemini API for processing. These are sent in real-time and are not stored by GhostLog after the response is received.
  • Subscriptions: RevenueCat manages subscription status. We only check whether you have an active "Pro" entitlement.

3. Third-Party Services

GhostLog uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Anonymous authentication and cloud data backup. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Gemini API: AI-powered food identification, meal suggestions, and fitness analysis. Gemini API Terms
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy
  • OpenFoodFacts: Open-source food/barcode database for nutritional lookups. OpenFoodFacts Privacy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your fitness data is stored primarily on your device using local storage. If cloud backup is active, data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore with security rules restricting access to your anonymous user ID only. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete the app. Cloud-synced data persists in Firebase until you request deletion. To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request deletion of cloud-stored data

6. Children's Privacy

GhostLog is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for removal.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact the developer via the App Store or Google Play listing, or email: ghostlogapp@gmail.com
