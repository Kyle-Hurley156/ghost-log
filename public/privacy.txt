Privacy Policy for GhostLog
Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect
GhostLog collects anonymous usage data, workout logs, and meal logs to provide app functionality. If you choose to link an Apple ID, we store your user ID to sync your data across devices.

2. How We Use Information
Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app (workout tracking, meal tracking, and AI analysis). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. AI Processing (Google Gemini)
When using the "Ghost Chef" or "Ghost Report" features, the text and images you input are sent securely to Google's Gemini AI to generate a response. Please do not submit sensitive personal identifying information to the AI.

4. Subscriptions
Payments are processed securely via Apple App Store or Google Play Store. GhostLog does not collect or store your credit card information.

5. Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support or using the in-app delete functionality (if available).

6. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the App Store listing.
